## üé• RECORDING TIPS

### What You Need:
- **Screen recorder**: OBS (free), Loom (easy), or Windows Game Bar
- **Microphone**: Your laptop mic is fine! Just speak clearly
- **Quiet room**: Close the door, turn off fans

### Before Recording:
1. **Practice 2-3 times** - Don't memorize, just know the flow
2. **Clean up your screen** - Close extra tabs, notifications off
3. **Have the API docs ready to paste**
4. **Test the generation once** to make sure it works

### Recording Tips:
1. **Smile while talking** - You can hear it in your voice!
2. **Don't be perfect** - A small stumble is fine, sounds human
3. **Use natural pauses** - "Let me show you..." [pause] "Here we go..."
4. **Show genuine reactions** - "Oh wow, that was fast!" feels real
5. **Talk like you're explaining to a friend** - Not a presentation

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- ‚ùå Reading a script robotically
- ‚ùå Using too much jargon ("leveraging synergies...")
- ‚ùå Talking too fast (breathe!)
- ‚ùå Forgetting to show your personality
- ‚ùå Being too formal

### What Judges Want to See:
- ‚úÖ You understand the problem (personal experience)
- ‚úÖ The app actually works (show it working!)
- ‚úÖ You're excited about it (enthusiasm is contagious)
- ‚úÖ It solves a real problem (not just a tech demo)

## üé¨ STEP-BY-STEP RECORDING PROCESS

### Day Before Recording:
1. **Test everything** - Make sure app works perfectly
2. **Prepare your demo input** - Have API docs ready
3. **Practice your script** - 2-3 run-throughs
4. **Set up recording software** - Test audio/video

### Recording Day:
1. **Take 1**: Just do it! Don't worry if it's not perfect
2. **Take 2**: Fix any major issues from Take 1
3. **Take 3** (if needed): Your safety backup
4. **Pick the best one** - Watch all takes, choose your favorite

### After Recording:
1. **Trim the start/end** - Remove any awkward beginnings
2. **Speed up slow parts** (optional) - If generation takes 30 seconds, speed to 10
3. **Add simple text** (optional) - "Doc2App" at the start, "GitHub link" at the end
4. **Export**: 1080p, MP4 format
5. **Upload to YouTube** - Unlisted or Public
