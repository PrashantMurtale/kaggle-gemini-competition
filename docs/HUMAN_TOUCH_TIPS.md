## Words to AVOID (Sound Like Corporate AI)

- Leverage/Leveraging
- Synergy/Synergistic
- Revolutionary/Game-changing
- Cutting-edge/State-of-the-art
- Unprecedented
- Paradigm shift
- Facilitate
- Utilize (just say "use")
- Solution (say "tool" or "app")
- Ecosystem
- Strategic
- Robust
- Scalable (unless you really mean it)

## Authenticity Checklist

Read your writeup. If you can answer YES to these, you're good:

- [ ] Would I actually say this to a friend?
- [ ] Did I share a personal experience/story?
- [ ] Am I honest about what it doesn't do?
- [ ] Do I sound excited (not salesy)?
- [ ] Would another developer relate to this?
- [ ] No buzzwords or jargon?
- [ ] Specific examples with real numbers?
